SkyLux App Policy

📅 Last updated: July, 2026

SkyLux is an all-in-one digital travel platform engineered to streamline global transit across our mobile application and official website. Our ecosystem bypasses the rushed stress of traditional booking by instantly connecting you with real-time, up-to-the-minute flight schedules and live pricing pulled directly from hundreds of airline databases.

At SkyLux, we prioritize your trust and are fully committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we gather, utilize, protect, and retain your data to provide you with a reliable travel planning ecosystem. By accessing our platform or creating an account, you consent to these operational data management practices. If you do not agree with the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy, please discontinue your use of our Services.

▶

1. Operating Entity & Data Administration

SkyLux is operated by the internal app administration team behind the SkyLux Mobile Application. Under standard digital data protection guidelines, our application ecosystem acts as the primary "Data Controller." This means that our app software, database servers, and system developers are responsible for managing, storing, and securing the personal information you share with us while planning your trips.

To fulfill your flight bookings, your data is securely stored and redirected to global travel leaders and trusted third-party providers, such as Trip.com, major international airlines, and certified reservation networks. These corporate partners process your information safely to finalize your tickets, issue boarding passes, and manage your travel itineraries.

If you have any questions about how your information is handled, or if you want to request data changes, you can message our team directly. To get in touch, please use the communication links provided in the "Help & Support" menu screen inside the application.

2. Our Core Services

SkyLux provides a streamlined digital travel ecosystem designed to simplify global transit. Our central platform features include:

  • Global Flight Search: Live queries across hundreds of airlines to find instantaneous routing options.
  • Fare Comparison: Dynamic price engines that analyze and secure competitive ticketing deals.
  • Instant Booking: Direct, integrated pipelines for flights, baggage selections, and seating layouts.
  • Itinerary Management: A unified dashboard providing live tracking, delay updates, and digital boarding passes.

Privacy Framework

All core privacy operational rules, secure direct credentials processing parameters, data sharing pipelines, and data collection protocols are comprehensively listed below.

Information We Collect

3.1 Information You Provide Directly

We process information across three primary channels: details you provide directly to our interface, operational diagnostics captured automatically during your session, and validation streams synchronized from trusted third-party global travel networks.

3.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account & Profile Credentials: Full name, verified email address, phone number, and optional profile pictures. Your account password is converted and stored exclusively in a secure, one-way encrypted (hashed) state.
  • Identity Verification data: Date of birth and nationality, processed only when explicitly mandated by international border customs or airline ticketing systems.
  • Travel & Manifest Specifications: Passenger names, routing destinations, departure/arrival schedules, historical trip records, companion profiles, and optional frequent flyer/loyalty program identifiers.
  • Flight Preferences: Stored seating choices, baggage selections, and specialized in-flight meal options.
  • Billing & Transaction Records: Billing addresses, transaction confirmation histories, and receipt ledgers. Full credit card credentials are never processed or retained on our application servers; they are managed entirely by our certified, secure third-party payment gateways.
⚠️
Important Security Note on Sensitive Data Frameworks:

Certain international travel bookings require processing sensitive personal elements—such as passport sequences, government ID numbers, medical accessibility requirements, or religious dietary choices. We handle these data fragments using rigorous isolation filters and execute transfers strictly to validate your passenger manifest with the performing air carrier. Do not share unsolicited sensitive data strings.

3.2 Information We Collect Automatically

Whenever you navigate our mobile application or web portal, our underlying processing engine automatically generates logs containing the following transactional metrics:

  • Hardware Specifications: Device architectures, active operating system layers, distinct network nodes, browser configurations, and unique mobile device identifiers.
  • Session Diagnostics: Real-time feature usage analytics, total screen runtimes, search query histories, and precise gesture tap coordinates.
  • System & Crash Reporting: Originating IP addresses, epoch server access timestamps, application crash dumps, and internal error profiles.
  • Spatial Location Coordinates: With your explicit permission, approximate or high-precision geographic telemetry is read to isolate nearby terminal hubs, display relevant fares, and optimize local travel insights. You can revoke location access at any time within your native hardware system preferences.

3.3 Information From External Partners

To maintain database consensus and prevent malicious activities, we synchronize additional validation inputs across specialized enterprise distribution channels:

  • Transit Consolidators: Live booking clearinghouses, global distribution systems (GDS), and individual airline carrier networks verifying reservation updates.
  • Risk Mitigation Nodes: Certified payment processors transmitting tokenized authentication metrics to preempt transactional fraud risks.
  • Single Sign-On Systems: Third-party corporate authentication providers (such as Google or Apple) sharing specific profile configurations authorized by your permission settings.
  • Analytics Syndicates: Aggregated demographic data pipelines, received exclusively in fully pseudonymized or de-identified data formats.

4. How We Use Your Information

We process and distribute your personal, behavioral, and transactional data fragments across targeted architectural layers to maintain core system operations and guarantee global travel transaction security:

  • Service Delivery & Fulfillment: Executing live airline database queries, processing multi-carrier ticketing, issuing digital boarding passes, and updating unified passenger dashboards.
  • Payment & Billing Operations: Managing secure financial clearances through third-party processors, validating ledger routes, and delivering automated transactional receipts.
  • Dynamic Communications: Broadcasting critical, real-time alerts regarding flight schedule disruptions, gate modifications, baggage carousel targets, and active customer care updates.
  • Experience Personalization: Recalling favorite cabin seat arrangements, remembering past trip destinations, and optimizing live pricing structures to fit your profile settings.
  • AI Travel Concierge: Powering the 24/7 background conversational assistant engine to parse travel questions, resolve support loops, and provide instant route recommendations.
  • Diagnostics & Optimization: Analyzing telemetry logs, monitoring core app runtimes, resolving code conflicts, testing prototype layout features, and scaling cloud performance maps.
  • Security & Exploit Preemption: Verifying registration criteria, auditing sign-on sessions, detecting suspicious script executions, and protecting corporate ledger balances against malicious entities.
  • Marketing & Promotions: Delivering tailored promotional offers, value bundle campaigns, and travel insights, fully subject to your individual choices and data preferences.
  • Legal & Carrier Compliance: Complying with corporate taxation regulations, fulfilling global accounting requirements, satisfying border security controls, and aligning with global transit directives.
